Geodetic and Control Data

Our Geodetic and Control Data consists of precise reference points and networks that form the backbone for all mapping and surveying activities across South Africa. This data ensures spatial accuracy and consistency for all geospatial datasets.

How to access the data: You can download geodetic control points and related datasets via the Metadata Catalogue or use the Interactive Map to locate and select specific control data of interest.
