Beacons

Title: Survey Beacons - Cape Town

Summary: Collection of survey control beacons used for geodetic referencing in Cape Town.

Keywords: Beacons, Survey, Geodetic, Reference, Control Points

Abstract: This dataset contains the official survey beacons within the City of Cape Town used by land surveyors and GIS professionals for geodetic referencing and cadastral mapping. These control points support spatial data accuracy across various projects.
