Beacons

Title: Survey Beacons - Cape Town

Summary: Survey beacons for geodetic control and referencing in Cape Town.

Keywords: Beacons, Geodetic Control, Surveying, CDNGI

Abstract: This dataset provides the locations of geodetic survey beacons within the City of Cape Town. These fixed reference points are essential for accurate mapping, cadastral surveys, and infrastructure development projects.
