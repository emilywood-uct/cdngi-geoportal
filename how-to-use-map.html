How to Use the Interactive Map

The Interactive Map allows users to visually explore spatial datasets hosted on the CDNGI Geoportal. This includes aerial imagery, topographic maps, elevation models, cadastral data, and more. Each dataset is organized by geographic grid tiles covering South Africa.

To use the map, simply zoom and pan to the area of interest. Click on any grid cell to open a side panel that lists the available datasets for that location. Each dataset category—such as Aerial Imagery, DEM, Beacons, Farms, Lease Areas, and Allotments—includes a link to its metadata in the catalogue and, where available, a download option.

You can also use the Metadata Catalogue directly to search for datasets by name, type, location, or keywords. The Interactive Map serves as a visual entry point for spatial discovery and easy access to authoritative geospatial data.
