Elevation Data

Our Elevation Data includes high-resolution Digital Elevation Models (DEMs) that provide accurate representations of terrain elevation and surface morphology. These datasets are widely used in applications such as flood risk modeling, watershed analysis, infrastructure planning, and environmental monitoring.

Available DEM Resolutions

  • 2m DEM: High-resolution elevation data suitable for detailed site analysis, engineering design, and urban planning.
  • 5m DEM: Medium-resolution data ideal for regional terrain modeling and hydrological studies.
  • 10m DEM: Coarser resolution data used for national-scale analysis and broad land surface assessment.

You can explore Elevation Data interactively using the Interactive Map or download elevation datasets and metadata from the Metadata Catalogue.
