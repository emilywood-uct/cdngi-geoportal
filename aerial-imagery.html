Aerial Imagery

Aerial Imagery consists of high-resolution orthophotos captured using aircraft and drone platforms. These images offer detailed and accurate visual representations of the land surface and are essential for land use planning, infrastructure development, and environmental monitoring.

Latest Data: The CDNGI Geoportal provides the latest aerial imagery for the Cape Town study area, captured in 2023. This dataset offers up-to-date, high-resolution visuals ideal for urban analysis, vegetation assessments, and terrain evaluation.

You can explore the Aerial Imagery using the Interactive Map or search and download metadata through the Metadata Catalogue.
