Landcover Maps

Landcover maps classify the Earth's surface into distinct categories such as urban areas, forests, grasslands, wetlands, water bodies, and agricultural land. These datasets are vital for monitoring environmental change, informing land use policy, and supporting biodiversity conservation.

The CDNGI Geoportal provides access to the most recent national landcover classification dataset, compiled in 2020. This dataset is derived from satellite imagery and validated with ground-truthing to ensure high accuracy.

You can access the 2020 Landcover Classification Dataset through the Interactive Map or find metadata and download options in the Metadata Catalogue.
