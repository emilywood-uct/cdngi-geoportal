Cadastral Maps

Cadastral maps provide authoritative information on land parcels, including boundaries, legal subdivisions, and property identification. This data is critical for land ownership verification, property valuation, and spatial planning.

The CDNGI Geoportal offers cadastral data for the Cape Town study area, which includes Farms, Lease Areas, Allotment Township, and Suburb boundaries. These layers support municipal management, urban expansion, and legal referencing.

View cadastral boundaries and land parcel details interactively using the Interactive Map, or download structured data and metadata through the Metadata Catalogue.
